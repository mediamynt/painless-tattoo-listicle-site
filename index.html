FAQ

Short answers that reduce hesitation.

Do numbing creams affect tattoo quality?

Sometimes they can change skin texture temporarily. Ask your artist ahead of time and follow the label.

How long does numbing cream last?

It varies by product and application. Many people report 1–4 hours of noticeable relief. Prep + timing matter.

What’s the cleanest way to test performance?

Keep the routine identical: same timing, same layer thickness, and record comfort ratings every 30 minutes.
